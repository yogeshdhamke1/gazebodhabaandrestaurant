WELCOME TO GAZEBO

Gazebo is a Dhaba & Family Restaurant located at Panvel - Mumbra Road in Rohinjan Village, near Reliance Petrol Pump. It's a complete family restaurant including Veg & Non - Veg dishes including Chicken Lollipop, Schezwan Noodles, Paneer Tikka Masala as well as starters, etc. We also include hukkahs for parties for friends and families. We have garden surrounded to our restaurant which is a best place to chill and hangout after a your lunch, breakfast or dinner. Resorts for the children, family as well as friends to enjoy your picnic or weekend trip with us.
